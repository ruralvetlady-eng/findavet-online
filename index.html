When to go to the ER

  • Trouble breathing, collapse, or unconsciousness
  • Severe bleeding or suspected broken bones
  • Poison/toxin ingestion (chocolate, meds, lilies, xylitol, etc.)
  • Persistent vomiting/diarrhea or bloated abdomen
  • Seizures, heatstroke, or extreme lethargy
  • Urinary blockage signs (straining/no urine)
  • Serious trauma, bites, or eye injuries
