When to go to the ER

(General info only. If you’re unsure — go now.)

  • Trouble breathing, collapse, unconsciousness
  • Severe bleeding or suspected broken bones
  • Poisoning (chocolate, lilies, xylitol, meds, rodenticides)
  • Persistent vomiting/diarrhea, bloated painful abdomen
  • Seizures, heatstroke, extreme lethargy
  • Urinary blockage signs (straining/no urine), especially male cats
  • Serious eye injuries, snake/spider bites, or trauma
