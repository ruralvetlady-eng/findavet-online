Poison Control

If you suspect your pet has eaten or contacted something toxic, call right away. The ASPCA Animal Poison Control Center is available 24/7 and can advise on next steps (fees may apply).

Why heartworm prevention matters

Heartworm disease is spread by mosquitoes and can be life-threatening. Prevention (monthly meds or injections) is far easier and safer than treatment.
